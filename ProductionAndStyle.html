Visual Effects To Persuade

The producers had a full game show (like America's Got Talent) with all the fancy show of effects and the slow build-up of suspense to see who gets to stay. There were refugees and their families that had to come in and “wow” the judges that pushed the suspense even further. I think that this was very effective way to show the terrors that refugees have to go through like how the government spends a lot of time and effort trying to make themselves look good while “helping” refugees by turning their lives into games.

Sound Effects To Persuade

Just like in ‘Visual Effects’, the music helps to exaggerate the suspense that is used. There are sound effects almost exactly like the America’s Got Talent show and the judges follow a similar questioning way as AGT judges do. I think what the producers were trying to do was make the PSA seem like it was actually America’s Got Talent with all of these effects to help people (most likely from the USA) to connect through how people feel when they lose in AGT and how people feel when they could lose their lives when rejected.
