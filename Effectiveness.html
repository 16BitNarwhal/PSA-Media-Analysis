Raising Awareness Goal

I think the PSA accomplished its goal of raising awareness and trying to decrease the number of refugees and asylum seekers being rejected by countries. After looking around the comment section of the PSA and another website that talks about the PSA, I noticed there were quite a few people that have realised how wrong a game show like this is (and there were of course people that actually thought it was real and asked what channel it was on). This awareness started in Norway by the Norwegian Organisation for Asylum Seekers and from the website, the awareness spread to Australia (and definitely other countries).
