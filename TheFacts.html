Statistics

The PSA/Media comes with a website (about the campaign). It says that 51.2 million people are on the run in the world and 17.9 million of these people are on the run outside of their home country. The Norwegian Organization for Asylum Seekers (NOAS) considers 1300 cases of refugees every year. 11500 people looked for asylum in Norway in 2014 where 7003 applicants were processed and accepted while 2393 applicants were rejected. This supports the main message as it says that there are millions of people that have similar issues where governments treat refugees’ lives like games.

Other Info

On the website (participants) there are stories of the ‘game contestants’ and how many of them have a high chance of being rejected. These real life issue examples are being treated like a game to governments, is used to help support the reason to care about helping these people.
